# Workforce Decision Support System

An integrated workforce data layer designed to move People Operations from reactive reporting to proactive decision-making.

## Problem

People teams often rely on fragmented data and static reports to answer workforce questions after the fact. Headcount lives in one system, attrition signals in another, and engagement data in a third, with no shared structure connecting them.

This creates delayed visibility, inconsistent metrics, and decisions made on incomplete information.

## System Design

The system consolidates core HR data streams into a unified analytical layer:

- **Headcount monitoring**: workforce composition tracking across business units, levels, and geographies, replacing manual snapshots
- **Attrition detection**: exit and tenure data structured to surface retention risks by segment before they reach executive review
- **Engagement integration**: survey and sentiment data linked to organizational structure, enabling correlation with turnover, mobility, and team health
- **Diversity tracking**: representation metrics tied to hiring, promotion, and attrition flows rather than isolated point-in-time counts

## What This Enables

- Early identification of retention risks in specific teams or populations
- Workforce planning grounded in actual attrition and mobility patterns
- Leadership reviews supported by consistent, cross-system metrics
- DEI accountability tied to pipeline outcomes, not just point-in-time counts
